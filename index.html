The basics

 

What does Dlavo Do ?

Dlavo is an on- demand alternative to your normal laundry, dry cleaning and fold experience. You do not may have time to do loads of laundry, fold your clothes or drive to the dry cleaner, or maybe you just dont like it or dont feel doing it. That`s why we came in.

We are a solution for doing laundry just by clicking on the app, you Schedule your laundry pick up, or your clothes being folded and set your personal preferences.

Then, one of our partners will arrive to pick up your dirty clothes, you just have to pick a size of bag to fit all your clothes in, and you will get it back the day and time you schechuled through the app. It`s that simple.

 

What are our hours of operation ?

We are proud to be serving you 7 days a week, from 7 am to 12 am, including holidays.

To create an account, you download Dlavo through your app store or play store. You will be asked to enter your phone number and email address, input your delivery and credit card information, and set your preferences. You can also join us as Dlavo partner to earn with us.
